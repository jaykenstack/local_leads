Frequently Asked Questions

When will I receive my tax forms? ▼

Tax forms (1099-K and 1099-NEC) are typically available by January 31st of each year for the previous calendar year. You'll receive an email notification when your forms are ready.

What's the difference between 1099-K and 1099-NEC? ▼

1099-K reports payment card transactions, while 1099-NEC reports non-employee compensation. If you earn over $600, you may receive both forms.

How do I update my tax information? ▼

You can update your tax information in the Tax Settings section above. Changes may take 2-3 business days to process.

Do I need to pay estimated taxes? ▼

If you expect to owe at least $1,000 in taxes, you may need to make quarterly estimated tax payments. Consult with a tax professional for advice specific to your situation.
